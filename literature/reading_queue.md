## Questions to answer in notes

For every paper, record:

- Is rate estimated or serialized?
- What side information is counted?
- Which training data and test split?
- Is code exact, reimplementation, partial or absent?
- What is sequential decode depth?
- Is the task evaluator used during training also used in testing?
- Does a language/generative prior permit semantic hallucination?
- What claim is transferable to OCR, and what is not?
