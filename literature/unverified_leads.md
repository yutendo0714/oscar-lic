# Unverified Literature Leads

These leads are deliberately excluded from `paper_registry.csv` until an authoritative paper page, publisher record, or stable preprint can be verified. An AI agent must not cite them as established work.

## Text-aware LIC with mask-guided transform

A title resembling **“Text-Aware Learned Image Compression: Preserving Text Readability with Mask-Guided Transform”** appeared in secondary-index/search results during the 2026-06-24 survey. No authoritative arXiv, publisher, proceedings, or clearly attributable official repository was confirmed in the bootstrap pass.

Required verification before registry inclusion:

1. exact title, authors, year, venue and DOI/arXiv identifier;
2. whether text masks are transmitted, decoder-derived or encoder-only;
3. actual-rate accounting for masks/positions;
4. datasets, OCR model and detector/oracle-box protocol;
5. official implementation and license.

Do not use a secondary profile page or an unattributed PDF as the sole source.
